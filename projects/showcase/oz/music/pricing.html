Pricing

Check out our pricing structure and reach out to our team for customized pricing options.

Service Fee

This pricing is based on the various costs associated with the recommendation engine, keeping track of song owners, and tagging songs. The cost can vary but it is typically no more than $20 dollars per song.

Song Storage

This pricing is based on uploading and hosting your song. Storage for the first 30 days is free. Storage after 30 days requires $1 per year per song.

Payment Processing

This pricing is based on the associated costs when checking out and paying for songs. This can vary but it is typically no more than 5% of the total transaction.
