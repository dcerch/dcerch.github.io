Your Greeting Questions

Greeting questions are your beginning and ending questions to your prospect. Here is where you name your BOT as an additional electronic member of your team and ask the first question you need to know - usually their name.

The end greeting question is where you will let the prospect know what will happen next on their journey with your business.

The decision question is where you get your prospect to inform you why they called. It allows you to ask them pertinent questions for the area of focus they need more information on. Example: "Are you calling to learn more about THIS or THAT?"

Your Decision Points and Questions

What are the areas of focus your prospects need to know about your business and what questions would you ask? For example, a real estate firm might ask if you are a "buyer" or "seller" with those two decision points. A generator contract may ask if you need "plumbing", "electrical", or "kitchen remodeling".
