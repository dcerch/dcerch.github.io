Kojima Name Generator

Overview

This name generator is an adaptation of Brian David Gilbert's Kojima Name Generator. The original 11-page worksheet that was used to create a Kojima name is now a webapp; slightly modified to automate the process. You do not need to figure out name alternatives, conditions, or categories. In this version, all you need to do is fill out the personal and Kojima information - the webapp will take care of the rest.

Instructions

Fill out both the personal information and Kojima information sections then click on "Generate Kojima Name" to get your results instantly.
